Comprehensive "Dental Care in Duluth, GA"

Bleicken Dentistry provides comprehensive dental care to individuals and families in Gwinnett County and the surrounding areas. We treat patients of all ages – from children to seniors – in a friendly and comfortable office environment.

Our Team is led by Dr. Matthew Bleicken, a skilled dentist with over 18 years of experience providing excellent dental care in Georgia. Because our practice is privately owned, we are able to focus solely on the needs of patients, and are not influenced by factors such as corporate quotas or insurance plan nuances. At Bleicken Dentistry, we strive to treat our patients as family.

Each patient at our office benefits from both an individualized treatment plan, as well state-of-the-art technology and a caring and compassionate staff. We routinely provide the following services:

  • Cleaning and Check-ups
  • Digital x-rays and imaging diagnostics
  • Fillings, bonding and tooth repair
  • Treatment and gum disease
  • Initial and replacement dental implants
  • Emergency treatments

Cosmetic procedures, including reshaping, colored fillings, porcelain veneers and teeth whitening

We look forward to meeting with you and your family. Please contact our office at 770-497-1045 or via email at info@bleickendentistry.com. We treat individuals and families residing in Duluth, Johns Creek, Suwanee, Norcross and throughout the Atlanta-metro area.
